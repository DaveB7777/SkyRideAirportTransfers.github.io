Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 September 2025

1. Introduction

AeroRide (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal details: Name, phone number, email address, pickup and drop-off details.
  • Booking details: Travel dates, times, passenger numbers, flight information.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, and analytics data (via cookies).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To manage and confirm your bookings.
  • To communicate with you via phone, WhatsApp, or email.
  • To improve our services and customer experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We only share information with:

  • Drivers assigned to your booking.
  • Service providers (e.g., email/hosting providers) under strict data protection agreements.
  • Authorities, if required by law.

5. Data Retention

We keep booking records only as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations, after which your data is securely deleted.

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent to processing (e.g., marketing).

7. Cookies

We use cookies for basic analytics to improve our website. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.
